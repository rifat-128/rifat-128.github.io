Rifat Hasan

Mechanical Engineer | Programer | Designer

I am a mechanical engineer with four years of experience working in the pharmaceutical industry as a project engineer. Throughout my career, I have gained expertise in various areas such as CAD design, system design, and have developed a keen eye for detail.

While working in the pharmaceutical industry, I had the opportunity to contribute to the design and implementation of engineering projects, ensuring their successful execution and meeting industry regulations. My experience in project management has allowed me to develop strong organizational and problem-solving skills, which are crucial in delivering projects on time and within budget.

Driven by my passion for technology and my desire to explore new avenues, I have decided to transition my career to the IT sector, specifically as a web developer. I have always been fascinated by the creative and dynamic nature of web development, and I believe my strong analytical skills and attention to detail will be assets in this field.

To pursue my goal of becoming a web developer, I have been actively learning and acquiring new skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other web development technologies. I am dedicated to expanding my knowledge and staying updated with the latest industry trends and best practices.

My background in engineering provides me with a unique perspective, allowing me to approach web development projects with a systematic and analytical mindset. I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead in the IT sector, and I am confident that my skills, combined with my passion for learning and problem-solving, will enable me to make a successful transition into this field. I am eager to contribute my technical expertise, attention to detail, and strong work ethic to the world of web development and contribute to the creation of innovative and user-friendly web applications.
